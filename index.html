The Photographer

Meet Laxmi Frames & Films Studio behind the camera.

Photography is not just a business but a passion. Our goal is to capture the beautiful, natural, and lively moments of life through the camera. Every individual has a unique story, and we love preserving it through their memories.

We work with our clients in a relaxed and friendly environment so that genuine emotions and memories come alive in every photograph. Preserving the best moments of your life for a lifetime is our true identity.

With over 3 years of professional photography experience, I've had the privilege of capturing some of life's most beautiful moments. From intimate portraits to grand wedding celebrations, I approach every project with the same dedication and artistic vision.
